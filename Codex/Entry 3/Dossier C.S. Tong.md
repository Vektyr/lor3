# Dossier C.S. Tong

SUBJECT PROFILE: C.S. TONG

// Full Name:            [[Chris Tong|Christopher Selm Tong]]

// Current Assignment:   [[Terms#REPNODE|REPNODE-M7]]

---

An Ring 2 Engineer that has been working on several [[Terms#CEDRT|CEDRT]] space stations
across the solar system. His relatives all served the CORE, most of them in
the [[CCS|CCS corps]] on [[Venus|Venus]] and [[Luna|Luna]].
[[Chris Tong|Christopher (Chris)]] has a strong character and is respected by his
colleagues and subordinates. He has been raised to the rank of Overseer
through his hard work and without any compromising circumstances.
Current position at the [[Terms#REPNODE|REPNODE-M7]] is not accidental - he has asked for a
transfer to that station due to some "personal affinity with Martian
tragedy". Although it might sound suspicious it seems that [[CIS|CIS (Core Intelligence Service)]] has not found anything that could suggest a hidden
agenda. At least that is the information that we were able to acquire.

---

[[Chris Tong|Chris]] is a tech-savvy person that has multiple hobbies, including drone
construction, which takes most of his personal time.
No immediate family is recorded, no wife or kids. No information about any
significant other as well. The guy is keeping his stuff to himself.
His colleagues say he plays poker with them from time to time but mostly
keeps things professional. Chris has been spotted playing virtual games on
several occasions (or at least using a VR headset) but no further details
are known.
