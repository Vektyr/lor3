# USOL


---

One of the major factions in the Solar System.

Based on the principles of freedom (in everything) they have embraced the "survival of the fittest" attitude in all of their ways. Driven by internal competition, as well as being united by common "outer" adversaries ([[SOYUZ|SOYUZ]] and [[Terra Mater|Terra Mater]]) they rely mostly on their space stations and asteroid bases, rather than colonies. Their plan is to utilize the Solar System to its limits without thinking of the distant future, and they are very good at exploiting that idea.
