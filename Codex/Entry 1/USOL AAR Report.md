**REPORT**

OUR SECURITY FORCE HAS BEEN TASKED WITH PREVENTING EXCESSES DURING THE PEACEFUL DEMONSTRATION ORGANIZED BY THE LOCAL CULT.

THE DEMONSTRATION WAS TETHERED TO A LOCAL HOLIDAY - **DAY OF THE FIRST COLONISTS**.

Local cults and "secret societies" have always used that day to stir discord between local government and the common folk, so we were well equipped to prevent any unnecessary violence.

**OUR FORCE CONSISTED OF 4 TACTICAL SQUADS:**

- **"WEASEL" SQUAD, [[Fussbender (surname)|SGT. FUSSBENDER]]**: Negotiation team, usually responsible for talking to the main instigators
    
- **"FULCRUM" SQUAD, [[Southland (surname)|SGT. SOUTHLAND]]**: Team of highly trained operatives tasked with neutralization of most violent targets
    
- **"OCULUS" SQUAD, [[Kenwood (surname)|SGT. KENWOOD]]**: Oversight and recon squad which was monitoring situation as a whole
    
- **"CERBERUS" SQUAD, [[Keen (Surname)|SGT. KEEN]]**: "Heavy" squad, the ones that always took it all head on.
    

We were positioned near the entrance to [[Sky Lift]] as a main and only defence of the structure.

Due to [[Precentor Mc Mitney|Precentor Mc Mitney]]'s orders we were not allowed to carry firearms, as the march was supposed to be peaceful. Yet, fully aware of what those people could do, almost each of us had a personal firearm equipped.

That day things started to escalate quickly.

By the mid day the main body of the so-called "parade" had reached the foundation of [[Sky Lift|Space Lift]]. The crowd was very much agitated, instigated by the local priests and basic instinct of destruction. Knowing that, [[Fussbender (surname)|Sgt Fussbender]] tried to find someone "responsible" to have a chat with and de-escalate. Sadly, it did not work, as the main instigators were staying further away from the main body of the parade, so there was no way to reach them. Due to that fact, the negotiation team was called back, but only 5 out of 10 operatives returned. The fate of the remaining people is unknown due to later events.

Since the crowd was deemed irresponsive, a **Fulcrum squad** was called in, to get the instigators. They were partially successful in capturing one of the most loud instigators. Sadly, this has become a trade as one of the operatives was "detained" by the crowd as well. His disability to blend in was deemed shameful but non life-threatening.

Yet, the fact of the operative's capture became a trigger for a more aggressive approach, and as the protestors reached the main entrance, the **Cerberus squad** was activated. In a few next minutes the "march" became a violent confrontation between the frenzied crowd and [[USOL|USOL]] elite counter-riot unit. The guys were buying us time.

We have radioed in the retreat request but to no avail - all communications were jammed because of the special decree, to prevent information spread. Apparently the higher ups decided that each critical location could be defended by a handful of trained and motivated operatives. One hour later those operatives had to withdraw into the [[Sky Lift|Sky Lift]] building and later on ascend to the docks, blocking internal lifts behind them. Yet, the protestors, not happy that they didn't receive a "piece of flesh" as they were shouting, decided to follow us to the top.

By the time they reached the docking area, our shuttlecraft was ready and waiting. All of the remaining team members had safely evacuated the premises.

While boarding the shuttle, [[Keen (Surname)|Sgt Keen]] reported that he had intercepted a signal that looked like either a heavily disturbed or fully encrypted communication attempt, but he was not able to decipher the correct protocol.

As the ship was undocking from the space dock, a large EMP wave (or something similar) cut through the station.

We saw one of the departing ships lose all power, which led to an uncontrollable spin near one of the docks. A few minutes later that ship crashed into the dock itself. The explosion triggered a chain reaction, which blew a major chunk off the station, presumably killing whomever was inside. About 20 minutes later, after our crew was able to reinstate power generation, we received an order to withdraw from the planet premises and meet with the incoming [[USOL|USOL 6th support fleet]].
