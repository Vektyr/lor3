# Dialogue with Aurana

## Aurana 1

Hello. My name is [[Cor3.gg/Codex/Dossiers/Aurana Strogova|Aurana Strogova]], I will be your handler for the duration of the Contract. First of all, please remind yourself of the terms and conditions written in it, we consider it crucial that you follow those closely. Your task is to uncover some secrets that we find … intriguing. We have several files in our possession that our intelligence service was able to acquire. Most of them are scans and photos. These files are not in the best condition, but, trust me, it took a lot of effort to get them. Each of the files contain some information that may or may not be useful in your investigation. But they all provide some context to the events that we are interested in.

We believe that things that are happening right now, the ones that you may have heard from the news, are somehow connected to those documents. Your main goal in this investigation would be the uncovering of such connections. I fully understand the “vagueness” of the task but consider it a test of your abilities and a tribute to the secrecy we operate in.

We, in turn, would provide you with several tools that you might find handy, but feel free to use any open source intelligence that you can find.

## Aurana 3

Finally, Good work on getting all the information. You have passed the test. Some may say it was not an easy one … not that i agree with that, but my client is pleased with your progress.

The point here is - we suspect that all those experiments and events of the past affect our current reality. By how much ? We do not know. And it is up to you to find out.

My client is confident that you would be able to handle this quest. But be cautious. There are people that would kill for the information you have now. The deeper you go the harder and more dangerous it will get. Failure is not an option.

Now, we are working on obtaining additional information on the matter, and I will share it with you as I get it. The only problem is - it's random. We do not know what and when we will get from our sources. So be ready for everything.

One last thing. My client thinks that it's not a single location described in all those files, there could be several. So if you find something - try to uncover that too. Won't hurt and might give us additional angle for the main thing.
