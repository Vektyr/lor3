# ASTROCROP

### Project "Dementer"

**PRESENTED BY:**
[[Astrocrop|Astrocrop]].
[[Cor3a/Dossiers/Alio Corvin|Dr Alio Covin]],
07.07.2182, message to investors

***

Project Demeter is something that we, in [[Astrocrop|AstroCorp]], are proud of. The substance that we have created, with the help of [[CORE Tech|CORE Tech]], relies on our research aimed at restoration of planet [[Terra|Earth]], or [[Terra|Terra]], as they call it nowadays.

We hope that the invention of this substance will open doors for any and all terraforming projects that were considered too dangerous.

Today, we are ready to proudly present to you one of the results of the "Demeter" project - a **Synthetic brassinosteroids Biostimulant, Version 4**, or as we call it - [[Terms#SBS4|SBS4]].

This substance is a biologically active compound aimed at increasing the growth rate of plants. The crops that have that compound applied to them show at least 84% growth rate increase.

SBS4 is considered non-invasive and safe to use on any type of crops, proven by extensive research.

Our biochemical division is eager to start the next phase of testing - on live animals. We think that feeding animals food containing our compound will increase their growth rates as well, or prolong their life span.

Although these hypotheses are yet to be proven practically, we expect that the potential benefits from this technology could include the increase of life expectancy for Humans as well. Needless to say, such experiments are not planned yet, and could only happen after several years of thorough pre-testing and approval from authorities.

Meanwhile, we are developing a vitamin solution that would include a dose of SBS4 to test on animals and make available soon for distribution to our partners all around the Solar System.

Please feel free to fill the "distribution priority" form to get in line for the first shipments !
