### IWT USE CASE

Recent field tests show that [[Terms#IWT|IWT tech]] can be used in advanced awareness systems. Combined with [[Terms#NIC power cells]], any [[Terms#IWT|IWT]] emitter can serve as a miniature radar, drawing a picture of a person with detailed precision. This technology has massive military potential.

Technically, all radio waves of specific wavelengths can be used in the same way, but [[Terms#IWT|IWT tech]] amplifies them, increasing signal range and clarity.

Basically, what that means is that [[CORE Tech|CORE Tech]] operators, equipped with specific emitters and receivers can manipulate all nearby equipment with [[Terms#IWT|IWT technology]] to emit radio waves that would create a 3D map of obstacles and refract the waves back to the listening device. This could happen with only a slight delay, almost in real time.

This means that the opposing forces would find no hiding spot, and only hard-shielded buildings or vehicles could offer protection.

Our [[CORE Arms|CORE Arms]] technicians are working on the ways to implement that knowledge into the portable multi tool prototypes. At the same time, our comrades from [[CORE Tech|CORE Tech]] are trying to create a defensive solution for the problem, as they think that it will only be a matter of time before someone from the major factions comes to a similar solution. For now, we propose to secure this information as much as possible, and lower potential emitting power of all devices using IWT, claiming either manufacturing flaws or "crystal degradation". We need to keep this in check, at least for now.
