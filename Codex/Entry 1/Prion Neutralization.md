"The experiments that we have made have finally paid off. We were able to block those negative effects of the modifier PrPs. Finally, after years of experiments it happened.

But we could say it's only because of the data we have received from [[CORE Tech|CORE Tech]] and their "advanced experimental site".

We have sent them about 50 samples of the mammal tissue, and not only that ... but just 5 were returned back, along with the data.

They claim that the solution they are providing would cure every protein-related disease in human history. I am hesitant to trust that, but maybe ...

The other side of the coin means that basically ALL mammal meat could potentially be used in meals. And I mean ALL.

I guess one can say it's a good thing, especially for the colonies struck with famine and relying only on external supply chains ... but still, this could lead to severe ethical problems. And I would prefer to not be a part of any of that at all.

This means that I will be turning my in research to the higher ups, and after that ... it's not my business any more.

Let the Lord have mercy on our souls."
