## [[Cor3a/Entry_1_The_Contract/Project Artemis|PROJECT: ARTEMIS]]
### **TOP SECRET**
**Informational brief, eyes only**

***

[[Project Artemis|Project Artemis]] was founded during the 2150's as a matter of controlling the freshly found colonies. Disguised as an "asteroid defence" program, it implied the deployment of missile platforms on the orbit of colonized planets and big enough space bodies (colonizable asteroids or even big enough space stations).

The deployment process was rather slow, due to encountering many setbacks because of inefficiency of missile technology in those circumstances.
At the same time, advances in the [[Terms#NIC power cells|NIC energy storage technology]] allowed [[CORE Arms|CORE Arms]] to propose the development of railguns, both suitable for space ships and orbital platforms.

The development started in the late 2160's with the construction of several r'n'd and test sites on [[Terra|Terra]], [[Luna|Luna]] and [[Mars|Mars]] (and also several smaller ones located on asteroids and space stations).

Although initially those sites shared information about their process, it was later decided that such communications would be closed because of a possibility of data leak. After that all of the research complexes became fully autonomous.

The main objective of all of those sites was to find the most durable materials for both the cannons and ammunition.
The early prototypes that used standard "[[Terra|terran]]" materials seemed to not be durable enough, as the prototype cannons were usually limited to a few dozen shots before critical barrel damage.
The ammunition used, mainly consisting of Tungsten alloy, seemed to be fine for most use cases, but was somewhat struggling with atmospheric entry.

During the operating period, several research centers have been lost due to failed experiments, pirate raids and terrorist attacks as well as general shut downs due to lack of results.

Considering that, [[Project Artemis|Project Artemis]] was mothballed in the early 2210's, with all data moving to archives.

The project was deemed obsolete later, due to [[CORE Tech|CORE]]'s paradigm shift towards economic and technological control over direct military intervention.

Recently, the project has been revived due to resurfacing of the information from the [REDACTED] complex, which was considered lost during the 2180's.

The [[Terms#LRMD|LRMD]] is a direct descendant from the Artemis project, yet its goal is to provide [[Perpetual Council|Council]] space ships with superior weapons capable of scaring off pirates, pillaging the inter-system stable trade routes.
