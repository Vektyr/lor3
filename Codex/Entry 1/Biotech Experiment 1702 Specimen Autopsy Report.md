Senior researcher
[[Konrad Malloy]]

has produced an autopsy on a specimen found at the -
REDACTED]
about 500 meters from the center. The first visual examination of the specimen suggested that it had been a monkey-like animal that somehow got delivered to the planet and died of natural causes, as no obvious signs of external damage were visible.
However, after the x-ray, m mapping of the body and full genetic scans were performed, the results showed "him" to be an adult human, roughly of the age of 50 years. The inside organs have been shifted and somewhat scrambled. The muscular system was all twisted and greatly enlarged and the brain tissue was only 20% of the normal size.
The radiation coming from the body was spiking at the nerve junctions and all equipment that could be affected by the resonance was producing quiet "humming" sounds while in a radius of 1 meter from the body.
[[Konrad Malloy|Doctor Malloy]] requested additional resources and equipment from the main facility to further his research on the matter. His preliminary conclusion was that the specimen was one of the first colonists that somehow got trapped in the area quite some time ago and was affected by different local emanations and biological conditions. He advised that the case would be given a "level 7 S" status thus restricting access to all documents.
