# SANDMAN AND SONS
# BUSINESS APPRAISAL.

***

"We all have gathered here to appraise the cost of the Company left to us by [[Solomon Sandman|Solomon Sandman]], a man, a father and an Undertaker.

Operating on mostly asteroid colonies, [[Solomon Sandman|Mr Sandman]] has forged a reputation of a reliable businessman that was always able to handle things, even if the client was hesitating.

Many respected the man, few loved.

Yet, sadly, in recent years his business started to dwindle, as a new technology called "corpse-less disposal" was invented. Whoever made that was either a genius or pure evil, but regardless instead of a problematic corpse people started to receive precious gemstones.

For [[Solomon Sandman|Mister Sandman]], in turn, that turn of events was fatal. No customers meant no money. So he had to get to the bottom of this.

Tragically, his choice of action was to infiltrate one of the [[Astrocrop|Astrocorp]] facilities. Needless to say, it has been well guarded.

The result came in a form of [[Solomon Sandman|Mr. Sandman]]'s death to, officially, an electric shock on a perimeter fence. That did not explain some missing tissue, but a hefty settlement sum was paid to the relatives for their troubles.

So, let us start with the list of remaining assets, financial obligations and potential profits ... "
