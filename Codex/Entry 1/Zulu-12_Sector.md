### Zulu 12 Sector Status

**UPDATE 2085**

**PRESENTED BY:**
[[Silvia Shepherd|Caretaker Silvia Shepherd]],
[[Astrocrop|5th Reclaimation Regiment]].
Redacted copy.

***

... Our recent advancements in plant genome modification provide us hope that a new breed of trees we are growing would be durable enough to sustain the harsh conditions of the Zulu sector.

Unfortunately, all of our experiments so far have failed due to extreme soil pollution.

The [[Astrocrop|5th Reclaimation Regiment]] suggests that the Zulu 12 Sector should be cleansed of all polluting equipment and machinery. We understand that some production in the area is critical to supply chains of the local concrete plants and light machinery plants, but we would like to state that adding a new, fast growing, forest would make a territory of about 300 square kilometers habitable.

We insist that creating a new habitat for our growing population would be much more "profitable" in the long run then draining all the scarce resources that are still stuck in the mud.

Alternatively, we would like to suggest investigating sectors 13 and 15 for possible testing sites for the new plants. Our initial scans indicate that chemical pollution levels there should be lower, although the radiation levels are still extreme due to the collapse of nearby nuclear stations.

Considering all the above, we would like to start sending scouting parties to sectors 12, 13 and 15 to further investigate the actual situation. Also, we believe that the Sustenance prerogative should be made aware of a potential [[CORE Tech|CORE Tech]] complex located somewhere in the area.

Meanwhile, our Biotechs claim they are almost prepared to present a new version of the Plant that would be radiation resistant (to a point, of course) AND would NOT store that radiation in itself. They are estimated to be ready for tests in 2 standard [[Terra|Terran]] months.

That is all for me.

Praised be the Mother.
