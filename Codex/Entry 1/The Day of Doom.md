# 2200 The Day of Doom

We still remember that day - though fewer of us remain.
It began with the first light.
The people rose to cast out the Oppressors at last. Freedom from [[CORE Tech|CORE]] and those [[USOL|USOL]] pets felt within reach.

The "March of Salvation" started near the old pit. [[Brother Mattheus|Brother Matheus]] spoke of hope, the Spirit, and the future. The regime was falling - factories silent, drones failing, the people awoke.

When the march began, thousands moved towards the [[Sky Lift|Haven Gateway]], the great [[Sky Lift|Skylift]] that fed [[USOL|USOL]]'s power from orbit. We aimed to cut their lifeline.
The path was harsh - mud, wind, sand - but no one turned back. At the base, we met the "police", the same ones who's stolen our kin. Rage broke loose. A riot.

The oppressors fled inside and sealed the lifts.

Then came the roar.

Lightning tore through the tower: part of it drifted into space. The air itself vanished - screams, silence, then darkness.

When I woke, the world was ash and ruin. [[Brother Mattheus|Matheus]] stood beside me, blooded but alive. We turned towards the sky.

The fight was won - but the cost was our blood.
