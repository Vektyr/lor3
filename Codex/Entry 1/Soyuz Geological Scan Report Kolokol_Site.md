### [[Andrej Makarov|Andrej Makarov]]

**SUBJECT** GEOLOGICAL SCAN, GRID
**DATE** E \[REDACTED\], AUG 2168

***

**REPORT**

Our crew was able to scan the area from low orbit with our new 3d deep scanner "Kolokol". The results show expanded ore veins of unknown origin that extend into the deeper planetary core. Although my colleagues think that this is some form of pure iron, I am hesitant to agree. The density of materials, if our scans could be considered correct, show something closer to Uranium or other dense metals.

But even that is not the problem. Somehow the scans show that this "vein" forms almost a perfect circle, or something very close to it, with a diameter of 3 kilometers. This could be considered a miracle.

That fact gives me a foundation to say that we are not looking at a mineral vein at all but rather some sort of a distortion field that disrupts our scans.

Sadly, there is no way for us to send a crew down there, as the [[CORE Tech|CORE]] forces are still present in the area, restricting access. Maybe our colleagues from intelligence can assist with that ...

If not, then we will keep scanning and prepare an unmanned solution, which could always have some sort of involvement deniability.
