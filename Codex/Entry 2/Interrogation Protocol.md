### RECORDING

**LOCATION:** -CLASSIFIED-, MAXIMUM SECURITY.

**INTERROGATING OFFICER:** \[REDACTED\]

**PERSON OF INTEREST:** [[Zachary Plox|Zachary Plox]], G-RACK ON-SITE CTO.

---

**OFFICER:**

Mr Plox, let's start with a simple fact. This is not a simple interrogation. You are not just a suspect. Consider this your trial.

You and your company have killed hundreds of people, and we are still estimating actual losses.

**ZACHARY:**

That was not me ! Not us ! I ...

**OFFICER:**

Stop this, please. I ask kindly, but just once. We have checked all the logs, all the system parameters and configuration files. We have cross-referenced them with the default configuration you were supposed to use ... and we have found changes, approved by you.

Now, would you like to say something about that ?

**ZACHARY:**

IT WAS NOT ME ! I DID NOT CHANGE ANYTHING ! ***smack*** Ugh !

**OFFICER:**

I told you, stop that. Speak like a man or this will get ugly, real fast. You have failed to deliver and you have set us up with that shit show. So if you continue to insist that you did nothing ... there are other people who can answer questions. More valuable people.

**ZACHARY:**

I understand, I am sorry.

**OFFICER:**

Finally. So, I repeat my question - do you know anything about it ?

**ZACHARY:**

Yes, we have experienced technical issues with the asteroid launch program, so the tech crew proposed some changes to the launch sequence that required my approval. I do not know what exactly they did.

**OFFICER:**

Oh, I know what they did, and I will tell you. They have altered the asteroids collision course so instead of passing near the colony they were supposed to hit it, causing massive damage and deaths of many influential people. But that did not work actually.

**ZACHARY:**

What do you mean ? Did the system deflect that attack ?

**OFFICER:**

Oh, no, sadly. Your "system", as you call it, is a copy-cat attempt to clone an early version of a [[Terms#Holoscene AI|Holoscene AI]], meaning it had all the bugs plus the ones that your ass-handed engineers added while trying to "fix it". I honestly have no clue how you were able to make it pass the inspection and where you got the initial code (we will deal with that later).

So instead of just failing, it basically crashed, inverting the programmed flight patterns and the asteroids, instead of crashing the colony, went straight to the ships nearby.

Accidently, the "defence" part of your system operated properly and destroyed MOST of the incoming asteroids. But that was obviously not enough as the ones that passed through headed towards the ships. Some of them crashed, making the crew panic and starting to maneuver. And then your "system" blew them up as the hostile targets. Which means that IFF did not work either, by the way.

So as you see, we know everything already, and you are alive for only one reason - I need to hear the names of those "engineers" that proposed the initial changes.

**ZACHARY:**

[[Simon Walls|Simon Walls]], [[Freya Kurkwurm|Freya Kurkwurm]], [[Sergey Plox|Sergey Plox]], and some guy [[Alexey (unknown)|Alexey]].

**OFFICER:**

Who is that [[Alexey (unknown)|Alexey]] ?

**ZACHARY:**

I do not know. He was transferred from the main office 2 days prior to the show.

**OFFICER:**

Did you check his credentials before allowing him access ?

**ZACHARY:**

Yes, but he came with a letter from the main office and we were in a rush.

**OFFICER:**

So you are saying that you have neglected security because of the tight time frame. Is that your defence ?

**ZACHARY:**

No ... yes ... I don't know. I am sorry ... please ... ***slap***

**OFFICER:**

Please don't test my patience. There is none left. You will be transferred to one of our facilities for ... additional questioning. Oh, and one more thing. There is no "Alexey", but we have found a manifest embedded in the configuration file. I will let you read it while you are in transit. Good luck.
