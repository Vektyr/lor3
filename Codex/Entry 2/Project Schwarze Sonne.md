# Project Schwarze Sonne

## Customer Data Archive

Mail correspondence between professor [[Gerhard Zonge|Gerhard Zonge]] and [[Samuel Packston|Samuel Packston]] describes delivery of a so-called [[Terms#Black Sun device|Black Sun device]]. Early accidental activation caused tremors, trance-like states and neurological distortion. Zonge still planned to present the device during a private gamma party, while hidden manual sections implied more dangerous parameters were intentionally protected.

## Gamma Party Incident

The party recording shows fifteen guests gathered within the required radius before activation. What followed was nearly ten minutes of screams, loss of control and apparent neural collapse. The device's own informator announced that all targeted individuals had been affected and the mission objective had been achieved.

## Internal Assessment

Follow-up reports make it clear that the operation was not meant to kill. Younger subjects were hospitalized, middle-aged subjects suffered severe neurological damage, and older ones broke down completely. The core conclusion was that the prototype had run in cleanse mode instead of controlled indoctrination mode. [[Feud (surname)|Doctor Feud]] was then ordered to keep experimenting until the effect became stable, repeatable and useful for covert mass control.

## Supplemental Brief

A classified project note places Black Sun on [[historian/vault/Locations/Ganymede|Ganymede]] and describes it as a microwave emitter built to rewrite human brain-wave patterns into obedience. Animal testing had already failed catastrophically, yet the project moved toward direct human trials using a modified power cell from a Martian facility. Feud's personal log suggests he no longer believed the device produced control at all, only erasure of self. ^a3da14
