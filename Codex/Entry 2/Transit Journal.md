# Transit Journal

Entry 1

Today my journey begins. The flight should take me straight to the Martian [[Terms#REPNODE|REPNode]], from where i will continue my investigation.
The reason i agreed to this is pretty simple - intrigue. I feel that i am getting myself into something big, and that worries me, but thrills at the same time.

The past investigations that i’ve done lead me to a thought that all of those events are somehow connected. Maybe not directly, and maybe just by coincidence, but still connected, as if someone very powerful is pulling all the strings, making stars align.

My employers - [[Terms#History Unfolded|“History Unfolded”]] seem to be worried somehow. Although i am certain that they have already established their presence on the station, or even on the planet itself, it seems that their “tools” are not suited for a further investigation.
Somehow, it feels like they are interested in me personally, for whatever reason.

This flatters me of course, but raises questions about the origins of such interest. My whole life, as i can remember, i have been a “rolling stone”. Never stayed in one place for a long time, never had any attachments to people.
I have been a security operator, a junior technician on one of the mining stations, a truck driver on Luna, then, again, an enforcer for one of the corporations, an assistant captain for one of the Terran’s trade route cargo ships and so on and so forth.
That one time i was even tied up in a difficult situation and had to herd cats. That was a weird one.
But all this life experience did not prepare me for becoming a PI. And yet, after i set up my resume and started looking for contracts as a Private investigator, i’ve received that job offer within 3 days.

This could look suspicious or totally coincidental, depending on how paranoid I would like to be.

Another problem is that I always have that feeling that something is missing. An item, a person, a memory … I do not know what. And as far as I can recall, that has always been an issue.
But since i have started working on those investigations things got worse.

I have tried talking to a remote medical assistant and even paid for a brain scan, but the results showed nothing uncommon, aside from traces of an old concussion that I have no memory about.

Could that be the source of all problems ? Might be. But my hunch, that i have been relying on a lot, tells me it’s not that simple. The fact that i do not remember big chunks of my past is not a thing to just throw away.

But why does that bother me so much all of the sudden … maybe it’s space. Too much time to think, I have tried to avoid that for quite some time …

Entry 3

Look what we got here. Looks like some fragments of a video file. But it’s not full so I guess opening each part would take an extra effort. And there seem to be quite a lot of them …
My best guess is that it might be some sort of an educational video for CCS colonists, or some historical stuff. But you’d never know - might as well be an R rated home video, so much weird shit i’v seen after all, huh.

Anyways, it would definitely take some time to unlock all of those and while it might be properly entertaining I think I would need to find something else to do, while maintaining a low profile on this ship.
