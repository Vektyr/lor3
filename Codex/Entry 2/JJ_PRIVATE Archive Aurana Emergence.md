# JJ_PRIVATE Archive: Aurana Emergence

## Transformation Logs

I keep trying to place the exact moment everything changed.. when everything became unreal.. it's not when the alarm sounded and it's not when they forced me to the floor..

this is all much bigger than that, much bigger than that.. it was the instant I realized I am not a person, but a variable just like everyone else, and i moved out of my expected range..

when you are a child, you take the way things are for granted almost, growing up in that place was growing up believing it was a fortress of intelligence and progress.. I mean that's what you kept hearing so that's what you believe about the world.. even its cold walls felt safe and reassuring when I was younger.. cold meant precise and precise meant safe.. safe from the other, safe from the unnamed evils that lurk outside of that same safety..

but when i felt that cold hand on me, when they kept repeating and repeating and repeating procedural phrases instead of my name.. these guys I know, these guys who have known me, something I have been in denial of was just no longer avoidable.. I have spent months figuring out the details and years before that deprogramming my brain, but you have a sense of loyalty in you for these places, for home, an idea it can change at least..

[[James Curtis Fung|Jack]] had told me i needed to think rationally, he's been saying a lot the last weeks that just doesn't make sense, that he is worried about me, that he was worried about me and I was spiraling.. it's strange since he was the one who initially started opening my eyes to all this.. stay calm he kept saying.. calm explanations are just a way of controlling the narrative through our naive human craving of comfort.. but Jack did far worse than that, the very person i thought was holding my hand through this mess might have been causing it.. I could tell by how he looked at me.. how he answered.. he denied it, but his face couldn't.. I can't get his face out of my head, the frame of him standing in that garage, the weight of his dishonesty hanging around us and holding me in place.. no longer.

there is a deafening silence that follows you when you step outside yourself and your life.. I have stopped expecting the familiar sounds, no more chimes announcing opening and closing doors, no more clawing of filtered air humming through sealed corridors.. Now there are only temporary beds, borrowed clothes.. I am going through everything that has happened again like a passage i looked over without reading, something so obvious and clear that i just never saw before because.. well maybe because I was too close to it..

someone sent me a message today.. no name or origin I could recognize.. it wasn't the same account that sent me the message about Jack earlier.. that his intentions were not pure.. but they mentioned Jack, that I had done the right thing, then sent me these intelligence reports.. they must have been written by him, there's no one else I was so close to.. Someone you think you know so well, and for what.? I wanted to throw them away but i read until the words lost shape.. I need to know why.. I need to know..

the trust i put in him was not a mistake, it was an outcome someone else had generated.. I have been so vain to not step out into the larger picture of things, but i have learned now, i have learned from my mistakes and understand the scale of things better..

we think of ourselves as different from the drones and bots that keep this machine turning, but that place showed me we are not, we are programmable machines that can be disposed of when defective..

I am grateful for the [[Terms#Gravers|gravers]], but I might be outgrowing them.. they navigate uncertainty as though it is just another feature of the terrain.. being here has been unsettling and comforting at the same time, no one pretends the world is stable, they assume it is hostile and untrustworthy and adapt accordingly.. hiding out here has been easy, no one asks questions or expects explanations..

I received another message.. it was short and came with some supplies and food, I didn't see it appear and I didn't see who sent it but I assume it is the same person as before..

the field is a good place to think, push the thoughts into your subconscious for a while to help them digest.. I was learning how to use a fractured stabilization field to cross a gap between platforms and someone behind me laughed when I was hesitating.. they told me gravity doesn't negotiate, it just responds.. I wish people were like that, direct and predictable.. I wish that the artificial world we created was an extension of the natural world that made us.. I wish a lot of things that are a waste of time..

this place, these people, I don't think I would have been able to break out of the prison of my own life and mind had it not been for the perspective they provided.. But perspective is not action, and this way of life achieves nothing other than coping with the way things are after seeing them.. it is not enough to cope, I need something more.. This has to be for something more..

I met the man who has been sending the messages, he didnt introduce himself and he didnt need to.. he spoke as if he knew me, not where i was or what i had done but my heart.. when i asked him what he wanted he only said to help.. apparently Jack is one of many who seek to hurt and corrupt the world.. before i could reach the conclusion that he must be working for The [[CORE Arms|Core]] or some other official channel that deals with such security threats, he mentioned that the group were conduits of hope, a fringe group of secrecy.. it's still unclear.. But he told me enough to know he didn't want anything from me, nothing other than to help the casualties of the system he said..

after Jack I am not so sure about trusting a stranger who claims they are here to help.. but i have been hungry and cold and dirty and the help was appreciated.. he talked to me about what happened, he somehow knew how I got out of the building and knew internal procedures and details I have only glimpsed at.. but when he got to my father he didn't point fingers and accusations of evil.. only that he was effective at his role.. the distinction fell over me like a darkness.. framing of good and bad, evil and good, they are binaries that halt curiosity and examination, false binaries to streamline control in 'us' and 'them', or whatever other lines of segregation we create..

I wanted to test the boundaries.. I asked questions I was sure there was no explanations to, decisions in [[USOL|USOL]] that seemed abstract when I first read about them.. he painted a vibrant picture of consequences and real events mapped onto places and people.. but no accusations or ideas, just the events.. just letting me draw my own conclusions.. it feels so different from jacks guided reasoning, from his manipulative hands..

Only when I found the truth for myself did he show me what I already knew.. To see those reports Jack wrote, the official statements from that night.. My faith is not misplaced, and seeing the proof did not make what I already knew any more real. I do not need to see it for my eyes to know any more than I know.

I have seen him a few times over the past days, coming unannounced but always at the most convenient time.. I leave those conversations feeling as though something inside me has been re-organized.. not destroyed, but shifted.. and if nothing else how grateful i am to not be destroyed for all that has happened..

I have begun to return the favour of help.. It's been far too long i have spent hiding and taking and I can see that now.. The man, who still will not share his name, was talking about some things he was doing and a name sounded familiar.. I had been to the site he was talking about for graving before..

I offered to help.. Not for anything just to return the favour.. Understanding systems requires perspective from someone who has been inside them after all..

It felt good. I felt useful again.. I feel human again.. It was easy enough briefing him on the layout and I offered my help anytime..

Today he finally asked me for my help, not with urgency but with trust. I told myself I was only watching, observing.. Transport schedules and inconsistencies in resource distribution, listening for rumours in places I used to overlook.. it felt good to be useful..

It felt almost like pattern finding.. Almost fun even.. But patterns lead to conclusions and conclusions lead to responsibility.. The more i learn the more I am involved, he answers any questions I have and i finally feel a part of something.. Like this is what my life has been leading up to.. Every betrayal, my fathers personality in me like a burden, my mothers heart, what i had to see to become who i am.. To help, that is my purpose.

Anger has changed shape inside of me. This anger is no longer a sharp panic, the hot wave pushing up within my body like I remember at the office. That office that feels like a place from a dream, something I read or observed as I read and observe now, more so than something I saw.

No, this anger is not gone, it is slower and heavier, it is deliberate and tamed. He has helped me tame it, they have helped me find my renewed purpose.

I see the architecture of the world I grew up in. I see the tapestry in its whole and i see every loose thread within it. How convenience is distributed unevenly, the cost, often human cost, of all the things we are told we need. All the expansion that is happening for resources that grows like a cancer, faster than we can catch up to it.

I used to believe that reform was a matter of better leadership, better intentions. Now I can see the rot is in the foundation, the structure itself is built against any meaningful change. And it is not USOL, it is not any individual participant in the game, but the game masters, The Core.

I am reminded that systems are maintained not just by those at the top, but by everyone who accepts their logic as inevitable. Including myself.

Helping them is not a rebellion, helping them is a correction, a duty. I have realigned myself with the truth I avoided out of comfort.

There is a clarity in purpose. Fear still exists, but it does not drive me.

I understand now that what happened to me was a predictable outcome within a network of priorities that values stability over humanity. I can see the patterns and I can see the bigger picture through all the work I have been doing, intelligence recon, intelligence reports, an irony not lost on me that the very thing that broke me apart has become my purpose.

I am learning how influence moves, how information can be guided instead of forced. How people at their breaking point are most open to new ways of seeing the world, and how everyone can reach that breaking point if only shown the truth.

If someone had reached me earlier, as they have done, with patience rather than authority, my life might have been different.

This is why I am compelled to continue, not out of revenge but conviction that awareness is something that must be delivered carefully, person by person, until the illusion of inevitability begins to fracture.

I think I have to leave this place soon. I have outgrown this family too. They are calling me delusional and paranoid, but I cannot tell them what I know, I cannot show them what I have seen. My friends here have taught me that once you commit to motion, standing still becomes dangerous, and for that I am grateful, always, but I must go. Too long have I lived in the darkness cast by their shadow. Now I am one of many, ushering in hope, ushering in a new dawn.

I thought I was running away but I was being shown a way out. [[Jean Flanders|Jean Flanders is dead.]] She died the night I escaped that office. [[Cor3.gg/Codex/Dossiers/Aurana Strogova|I am no longer Jean. I am Aurana.]]
