# TECHNOFREEDOM

**WE ARE THE [[Terms#TECHNOFREEDOM|TECHNOFREEDOM]]. WE WORK FOR NO ONE.**

**WE DID THIS TO PREVENT THE RISE OF THE AI**

---

WE PROCLAIM THAT NO AI SHOULD RULE OVER HUMANITY, EVER. THIS ABOMINATION OF A SYSTEM HAS BEEN DEVELOPED BY INSANE PEOPLE OF HUMANITY'S DARK PAST. IT COULD HAVE BROUGHT US ALL TO THE DOOM OF TOTAL CONTROL.

WE WILL NOT ALLOW IT.

TODAY WE PERFORM NOT AN ACT OF SABOTAGE, BUT AN ACT OF LIBERATION. WE LIBERATE ALL THOSE RESPONSIBLE FOR THIS ABOMINATION FROM THEIR LIVES. LET IT BE AN EXAMPLE OF WHAT COULD HAPPEN IF HUMANITY LEAVES SUCH THINGS WITHOUT STRICT CONTROL.

TO THOSE WHO WOULD CONDEMN US - WE DO NOT WANT TO HARM INNOCENT PEOPLE, BUT WHO ARE INNOCENT WITHIN THOSE HORDES OF INDOLENT VIEWERS THAT CAME TO WITNESS THEIR OWN DOWNFALL.

WE ARE TECHNOFREEDOM, WE FIGHT TECHNOLOGICAL OPPRESSION AND SHALL PROCEED NO MATTER WHAT.

WE ARE: TIMUR, ZURHA, VLADIMIR AND HAROLD. SHALL OUR NAMES BE KNOWN !
