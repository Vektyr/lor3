# Private Club Personnel File — James "Jack" Curtis Fung

## Dossier

The personnel file presents Jack as a decorated former [[USOL|USOL marine]] and asteroid-belt veteran, but the unofficial section paints a darker picture: anti-riot work, deep-blue operations, strong weapons aptitude and almost no surviving social ties. Psychological screening flags PTSD and strange memory instability, including stories about events he could not have witnessed.

## Interrogation

Internal Affairs questioned Jack after he failed to deliver a [[Jean Flanders|girl]] to [[Private Club PMC|Private Club handlers]]. During interrogation he was injected with a truth-inducing substance by [[Donovan (surname)|Donovan]]. Under chemical pressure he refused to disclose the girl's location, but one answer slipped through: [[Terms#QQ Energy|QQ Energy]]. Afterward the interrogator ordered him transferred to [[Terms#Camp Vernon|Camp Vernon]] and all evidence on site destroyed.
