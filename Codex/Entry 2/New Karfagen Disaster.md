## NEWS ARCHIVE : LIVE BROADCAST RECORDING TRANSCRIPTION - “NEW KARFAGEN DISASTER”.

**[[historian/vault/Dossiers/Vince Darken|Vince Darken]]:** This is Vince Darken, [[Terms#SNN|SNN]], reporting from a New Karfagen asteroid colony.

We are here today to witness the new automated defence system presented by [[Terms#G-RACK|G-Rack]] in collaboration with [[Terms#ROSTEK|ROSTEK]].

It’s very crowded here today as both companies have invited their partners to witness their triumph. The event is broadcasted live throughout the solar system !

We managed to get a hold of the ROSTEK representative - [[Malkolm Malinovsky|Malkolm Malinovsky]] !

**Vince:** Malkolm, would you like to tell us about that defence system ? What is so special about it ?

**Malkolm:** Yes Vince, but first of all - welcome to our show everyone ! So, this is not just some “new” defensive system - this is a control system for proximity monitoring and fast response interception ! This system allows the operators to monitor more than 2 000 simultaneous threats and neutralize all of them at the same time.

**Vince:** Sounds great, but can you get into more technical details ?

**Malkolm:** Um, a bit, I guess it’s a commercial secret, after all ! What would you like to know ? I will answer if I can.

**Vince:** What weapons can this “system” control ?

**Malkolm:** Any type of. From basic autocannons or even machine guns to missile and laser systems. There is no difference, the controlling mechanism will adapt to anything present.

**Vince:** Can you describe the controlling mechanism actually ? What is it ? A scripted system, an operated module or maybe even AI ?

**Malkolm:** Ha Ha ! No, you know that AI is mostly prohibited, especially in military systems. And although there are some ways to pass that, we, as a responsible company, do not abuse that.

**Vince:** Okay, but can you answer the question please ?

**Malkolm:** I would love to, but as you see it’s already starting. Better to just wait and see by yourself I guess !

**Vince:** Right. Thank you for your time then. All right ladies and gentlemen - it seems it’s time to watch the show ! Let us proceed to the control room, where all the magic will happen.

**[[Cor3a/Dossiers/Zachary Plox]]:**

Ladies and gentlemen, I present to you the "[[Operation Aquarium Log|Aquarium]]". An all-seeing perimeter defence system that detects targets in a 40 kilometer radius and is interference - resistant.

In a moment, we will start the fire test, using different weapon types at different ranges. The whole process would be monitored by several dozen cameras spread out the whole asteroid as well as your streaming cameras strategically placed at the most scenic locations !

We would be testing our weapons on the dummy targets presented by an artificial asteroid shower which is 100% controllable and poses no threat to this colony OR your docked or parked outside space ships.

We are also accompanied by the several cruise liners that have brought tourists to watch the show, and we definitely do not want something bad to happen to them !

So, without any further delay - let us start !

Operator, please launch the meteorites and activate the system in proximity defence mode !

**Operator:** Sir, asteroids launched, system operational, tracking 1200 fast moving targets.

**Zachary:** Very well - activate defense.

**Operator:** All systems green.

**_buzzing sounds and trembling_**

As you can hear, the defensive systems are starting to work. Long range lasers, medium range missile systems and short range autocannons and machine guns - everything is working fine. They are ready to track and destroy any moving target within their control zone.

**Vince:** Sorry sir, did you say ANY ? Because that cruise ship has just started moving.

**Zachary:** What ? Operator !?

**Operator:** System has detected movement ! The liner has been hit by a small asteroid and is starting to maneuver away !

**Zachary:** Tell them to stop ! Disable the system !

**Operator:** I can not sir ! The system has locked the target and is figuring out firing solutions !

**Zachary:** WHATEVER YOU HAVE TO DO - DO IT ! NOW ! STOP IT !

**Operator:** I ... can not !

**_sounds of weapons firing_**

**_Blinding explosion outside_**

**Vince:** You have seen it all ladies and gentlemen. There is nothing more to show. Our duty as human beings right now is to try and help rescue people from that cruise ship. There were hundreds of people ...

**Zachary:** This was a sabotage ! Sabotage, you hear ! Everyone should know - it is because we helped the [[CORE Tech|CORE]] ! THEY did not want that to happen - they threatened us ! I will tell the truth ! Everyone will know ! Sabotage !
