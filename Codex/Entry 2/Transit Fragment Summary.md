# Transit Fragment Summary

## Recovered Package

The timeline disk is not a polished record set. It is a deliberately messy package of videos, images and audio slices that had to be reconstructed during transit to [[Mars|Mars]]. That framing matters, because it turns the fragments into an active briefing rather than passive background material.

## Narrative Use

Combined with the transit journal, the fragment set marks the shift from the initial contract into the Martian phase of the investigation. The player is no longer reviewing old evidence from a distance; they are carrying unstable archival material into the next theater where those past events are expected to connect with present actors.
